TRIBUTE TO SPY HUNTER

- HOW TO PLAY

	In this game we have to stay the most time alive in the road.
	To control the car we have the following buttons:
	- A & S: Move left & right
	- W & S: Up & Down Gear
	- H: Gun Shot
	- j: Throw Oil
	- K: Throw Spray
	- L: Lunch rocket
	- F1: Show Colliders
	- F2: Show Road
	- F3: God Mode
	- F4: Full Weapon Mode
	- F5: Ambient Forest
	- F6: Ambient Desert
	- F7: Ambient Grey
	- F8: Ambient Green
	- F9: Ambient Snow
	- F10: Live Up
	- F11: Live Down
